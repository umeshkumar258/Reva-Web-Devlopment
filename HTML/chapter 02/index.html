Fruits Information

Apple

*An apple is an edible fruit produced by an apple tree. Apple trees are cultivated worldwide and are the most widely grown species in the genus Malus.

Banana

*A banana is an elongated, edible fruit produced by several kinds of large herbaceous flowering plants in the genus Musa.

Cherry

*A cherry is the fruit of many plants of the genus Prunus, and is a fleshy drupe (stone fruit).

Date

*The date is the fruit of the date palm tree, which is grown in many tropical regions of the world. Dates have become quite popular in recent years.
